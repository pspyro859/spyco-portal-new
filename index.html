☁️ Google Drive

Manage your Drive files directly from the portal

Connect Google Drive

Connect your Google Drive account to browse, upload and organise files with SPY COMMS naming — all from within the portal.

To connect Google Drive you need a free API key.

It takes about 5 minutes. Here's how:

  1. Go to console.cloud.google.com
  2. Create a new project called Spyco Portal
  3. Enable the Google Drive API
  4. Create an OAuth 2.0 Client ID (Web Application)
  5. Add portal.spyco.com.au as an authorised origin
  6. Copy your Client ID and paste it below
